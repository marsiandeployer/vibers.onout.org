Who We Are

A small product team building web services and Telegram bots. We write code with AI (Claude Code, Cursor) — it's our core workflow. Now we need people to test what gets shipped.

Two Ways to Earn Tokens

1

Flow Testing (Stable Income)

Every task comes with a checklist — steps to verify after deploy. Walk through each step, mark it "Approved" or "Failed" with details. You get tokens for every completed flow, whether you find a bug or not.

Simple flow (3-5 steps) 5-10 tokens
Medium flow (6-10 steps) 15-25 tokens
Complex flow (multi-device, integrations) 30-50 tokens

2

Bug Bounty (Pay Per Result)

Find a bug → get rewarded. The more critical the bug, the bigger the bounty. You can hunt bugs anytime, not just during assigned flows.

Cosmetic (layout, typo) 5-10 tokens
Functional (broken button, form fails) 20-50 tokens
Critical (data loss, security, payments) 100-500 tokens

About Tokens

Rewards are paid in project tokens. Token price is floating and tied to the project's growth. Think of it like equity in a startup — the earlier you join, the more upside potential.

  • Tokens are real project tokens on blockchain
  • You can hold, trade, or convert them
  • Early testers get better rates as the project grows
  • Transparent payout history for every participant
